# Sector Expansion Pack
A mod for Starsector that adds new ships, weapons, hullmods, missions, exploration content and more.

# Features
- New Bar and contact Missions
  - Search and Rescue Missions
    - Save a person from different situations. Rewards you with high reputation and a chance to hire the saved person
  - Artifact Incursion Missions
    - Steal a colony item from a rival faction. Rewards you with high reputation and credits or high reputation and the artifact itself
  - Hyperspace Anomaly Research
    - Look for anomalies in hyperspace and gather research data. Rewards you with credits and hyperspace topology data
  - Fleet Escort Mission
    - Escort a fleet from different situations. Rewards you with high reputation and credits or kill the fleet for its loot
- New sensor ghosts
  - ||A ghost that will trigger hyperspace storms||
  - ||A ghost that will pacify hyperspace storms||
  - ||A ghost that will eat a nearby fleet||
- New campaign events
  - Expedition fleets
    - Factions will send fleets beyond the core worlds to recover an unknown artifact. Follow or intercept the fleet and claim the artifact yourself or leave it alone and let the faction claim it instead
  - Incursion fleets
    - Factions will send fleets to raid an artifact from a rival faction. Follow or intercept the fleet and claim the artifact yourself or leave it alone and let the faction claim it instead 
- New industries
  - ||Matrix catalyst is a structure that allows you to merge or split AI cores to higher or lower-tier variants (Destroy a remnant nexus to obtain)||
- Second-in-Command aptitudes
  - Purist is an aptitude that forces you to play a specific ship design type. Skills from the aptitude increases the stats of that ship design type
  - Eclectic is an aptitude that forces you to play a fleet with a variety of ship design types. The more ship design types in the fleet the stronger the effects of the skill becomes

## Installation
1. Download the latest update and open zip file
2. Extract files to mods folder of your starsector directory
    - Default Starsector directory "C:\Program Files (x86)\Fractal Softworks\Starsector"
3. Run the game and click mods button and activate the mod

## Compatibilities
- Second-in-Command - Adds 1 new aptitude.
