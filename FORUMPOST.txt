[size=12pt][color=red]Warning:[/color][/size]
Due to its alpha state some features may not be fully fleshed out,
crashes may happen from time to time, dialogues maybe inconsistent,
and finally mod updates may not be compatible with each other

[size=12pt][color=red]Compatibility:[/color][/size]
Should be compatible with most mods
Safe to add from an ongoing campaign
Unsafe to remove from an ongoing campaign

[size=12pt][color=red]Integrations:[/color][/size]
Second-In-Command - Adds 2 new aptitudes
LunaLib - Has native support for mod settings
