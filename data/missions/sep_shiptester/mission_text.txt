Location: Somewhere in the Muskavar Sector
Date: Unknown

At the edge of Muskavar, where the last starlight gives way to the abyss, a mercenary shipyard drifts in silence. Shielded by debris and secrecy, it serves as a testing ground for experimental hulls and weapons too unstable or forbidden to be built elsewhere. Some whisper it survives only through the quiet funding of powerful factions who prefer their involvement unseen.

Here, ships with unstable flux cores, reactors built from lost Domain blueprints, and outlawed weapons are tested at great cost. Wreckage often vanishes into the abyss, proof of failures best forgotten. Yet for mercenaries, the promise of unmatched firepower and the favor of hidden patrons make Muskavar’s edge both a forge of ambition and a graveyard of ships.
